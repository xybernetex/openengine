### Top 3 Programming Languages in 2025

| Rank | Programming Language | Key Influences                    |
|------|----------------------|-----------------------------------|
| 1    | Python               | AI integration, Data Science      |
| 2    | JavaScript           | Web Development, Versatility      |
| 3    | Java                 | Enterprise Applications, Stability|

### Summary

- **Python**
  - Dominates due to AI and data science applications.
  - Continues to be favored for its simplicity and readability.

- **JavaScript**
  - Essential for web development.
  - Versatile across different platforms and frameworks.

- **Java**
  - Preferred for enterprise-level applications.
  - Known for its stability and scalability in large systems.

- **Overall Trends**
  - AI tools are increasingly influencing coding practices.
  - Future trends may shift as AI and technology evolve.
